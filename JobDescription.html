UX designer/Graphic designer

Salary: £30,000

Hours: 30h/week

Job Brief:

We are hiring a UX designer/graphic designer to create visually appealing and user-friendly designs for our web development projects.

Responsibilities:

  • Create wireframes, user interfaces, and graphics for web development projects
  • Work closely with the development team to ensure effective implementation of designs
  • Stay up to date with industry trends and design tools

Requirements and skills:

  • Bachelor's degree in graphic design, web design, or a related field
  • Proven experience creating wireframes, user interfaces, and graphics for websites and web applications.Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is a plus
  • Strong understanding of user experience principles and design tools such as Sketch, Adobe Creative Suite, and InVision
  • Ability to work well in a team environment and communicate effectively with clients and team members
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously

Front-End Developer

Salary: £45,000

Hours: 32h/week

Job Brief:

The front-end developer will create and maintain web applications, work with designers, collaborate with the back-end team, and stay up to date with industry trends.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain web applications
  • Collaborate with designers and back-end developers
  • Ensure that designs are effectively implemented
  • Stay up to date with industry trends and technologies
  • Ensure that front-end and back-end systems are integrated seamlessly

Requirements and skills:

  • Bachelor's degree in computer science, web development, or a related field
  • Strong experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience with front-end development frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js
  • Familiarity with version control systems such as Git
  • Ability to work well in a team environment and communicate effectively with clients and team members
  • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills
  • Experience with responsive and mobile-first design principles
  • Understanding of back-end development and API integration
